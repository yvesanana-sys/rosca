## What is CircleFi?

CircleFi takes the age-old tradition of community savings circles and makes them secure, transparent, and accountable. Members contribute monthly to a shared pot, and each round one member receives the full pot. No banks, no middlemen â€” just your community.

**Hybrid Architecture:** Pay through GCash, Maya, or bank transfer. Every transaction is verified and recorded on the blockchain for full transparency.
