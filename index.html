◎ Mission & Vision

An ancient idea.
A modern platform.
For every community.

For centuries, communities around the world have pooled their money together, taking turns receiving a lump sum that none of them could easily save alone. It worked then. It works now. CircleFi makes it safer, smarter, and open to everyone, whether you're in Manila, Nairobi, Lagos, or London. No bank account required. No credit score. Just your community and a platform that keeps everyone honest.

Our Mission

Give every person access to safe, dignified community savings wherever they are in the world.

Community savings circles have worked for generations because people trust each other. We're not replacing that trust. We're protecting it. CircleFi layers modern technology on top of a tradition that already works. Every member gets a transparent record, every manager builds a verifiable reputation, and every payment leaves a traceable trail. Dignified finance for everyone, whether you have a bank account or not.

Our Vision

A world where community is the most powerful financial institution you'll ever belong to.

Banks serve customers. CircleFi serves circles. We envision a world where savings, credit, and financial opportunity flow through the trust networks people already have, not through institutions that exclude them. No minimum balance. No branch visit. No rejection letter. Just your people, your commitment, and a platform that makes group saving as natural as sending a message.

The Problem & Solution

What is CircleFi?

Informal savings circles exist in every culture. The idea is brilliant but the execution has always been the problem. CircleFi keeps everything that makes them work and fixes everything that makes them fail.

🚫

The Old Way

Paper-based tracking, no accountability, organizers disappear with the pot, and no recourse when someone defaults. Trust is assumed, not verified. One bad actor can wipe out an entire group's savings and there is nothing anyone can do about it.

✨

The CircleFi Way

Smart Vouch accountability, real-time payment tracking, instant digital payments, manager career paths, and a Conflux social community. All of it backed by a tamper-proof community ledger. Trust is earned, visible, and backed by real stakes.

📒

Community Ledger Model

CircleFi never holds your money. All payments flow through licensed financial partners. Every transaction is recorded in a tamper-proof community ledger, permanently auditable, independently verifiable, and owned by no single person or institution.

🌐

Built for Every Community

Whether your savings circle is called paluwagan, susu, tontine, gye, or partners, CircleFi is built for you. Contribute from anywhere in the world. Your currency, your community, your rules.

Core Features

Built for Real Communities, Everywhere

Every feature solves a real problem we heard from savings circle members and organizers across the world, from informal neighborhood groups to cross-border diaspora circles.

🤝

Smart Vouch

New members need a voucher who posts a real financial bond. Consistent on-time payments releases the bond as a reward. Risk is shared, trust is earned, defaults are deterred.

📈

Discount Bidding

Members can bid to receive the pot early by offering a discount. The highest bidder wins the early payout. That discount then gets split as dividends among all other members. Everyone benefits.

🌀

Conflux Social Feed

CircleFi's built-in social network. Follow managers, share milestones, explore trending circles, and build your community reputation. All inside the app.

🛡️

Default Protection

Three resolution paths when someone defaults: Lucky Takeover, Restructure, or Trust Bond Seizure. Circles continue. Members are protected. No one loses everything.

⭐

Member Trust Tiers

Starter → Member → Trusted → VIP. Your tier unlocks direct circle access, higher contribution limits, and exclusive circles. Your reputation is permanent, verifiable, and portable.

💬

Circle Chats & DMs

Auto-created group chat per circle. Managers can pin reminders and announcements. Direct messages between members and manager. At-risk members are flagged for early attention.

📒

Community Ledger Trail

Every contribution, payout, and vouch bond is recorded in an immutable community ledger, cryptographically secured and independently verifiable. No one can alter the history. The record always tells the truth.

📍

Geo-Discovery

Find open circles near you. Trust is local. Joining a circle with people in your community makes accountability real and personal. Filter by type, amount, and duration.

🏢

White Label Entities

Cooperatives, NGOs, and financial groups can operate their own branded CircleFi instance with their own circles, managers, analytics, and role structure.
