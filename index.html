The Problem & Solution

What is CircleFi?

Traditional paluwagan relies on blind trust — and when trust breaks, everyone loses. CircleFi digitizes the entire system with real accountability, transparent payments, and community-owned trust scores.

🚫

The Old Way

Paper-based tracking, no accountability, managers disappear with the pot, no recourse when someone defaults. Trust is assumed, not verified. There's no paper trail, no protection, no community network behind it.

✨

The CircleFi Way

Smart Vouch bonds, real-time payment tracking, GCash & Maya contributions, manager career paths, Conflux social community, blockchain-verified every step. Trust is earned, visible, and backed by real stakes.

🔐

Treasury Rail Model

CircleFi never holds your money. Contributions route through BSP-licensed partners — Coins.ph Enterprise for amounts under ₱1,500 and Netbank BaaS for larger amounts. You pay in pesos. Every peso is accountable.

🌏

Built for OFWs Too

Filipino workers abroad can join and contribute to circles back home via PHPC stablecoin through Coins.ph. Your family circle, your community circle — you stay connected even from abroad.

Simple Process

How It Works

From joining your first circle to receiving your payout — everything is designed for transparency, accountability, and bayanihan.

1
Join

Find a Circle & Get Verified

Browse circles near you or join one via a manager invite link. New members complete a KYC check (government ID + selfie). Members with a Trusted or VIP rating join instantly. New or lower-rated members need a voucher who posts a Smart Vouch bond — real accountability from day one.

2
Contribute

Pay via GCash, Maya, or Bank Transfer

Every contribution generates a Smart Reference ID (PAY-YYMMDD-C{Circle}-U{Member}) traceable across all systems. Payments under ₱1,500 route via Coins.ph. Larger amounts go via Netbank BaaS. Every transaction is hashed to Polygon.

3
Bid (Optional)

Bid for an Early Payout

Need your money early? Submit a Discount Bid. The member who offers the highest discount wins the pot ahead of schedule. The discount is split as dividends among all other members — reducing everyone's next contribution.

4
Receive

Get Your Payout — Directly & Instantly

When your cycle comes, you receive the full pot minus the manager fee (2–3%) sent directly to your GCash, Maya, or bank account via Netbank BaaS. Your payout is logged in the virtual ledger and hashed to the blockchain — fully auditable.

Core Features

Built for Real Filipino Communities

Every feature solves a real problem we heard from paluwagan members and managers across the Philippines — and from OFWs who wanted to stay connected.

🤝

Smart Vouch

New members need a voucher who posts a real financial bond. 5 consecutive on-time payments releases the bond as a free contribution reward. Risk is shared, trust is earned.

📈

Discount Bidding

Members can bid to receive the pot early by offering a discount. The highest bidder wins the early payout. The discount is split as dividends among all other members.

🌀

Conflux Social Feed

CircleFi's built-in social network. Follow managers, share milestones, explore trending circles, and build your community reputation — all inside the app.

🛡️

Default Protection

Three resolution paths when someone defaults: Lucky Takeover (replacement member), Restructure (circle downsizes), or Trust Bond Seizure. Circles continue.

⭐

Member Trust Tiers

Starter → Member → Trusted → VIP. Your tier unlocks direct circle access, higher contribution limits, and exclusive circles. Your reputation is permanent and verifiable.

💬

Circle Chats & DMs

Auto-created group chat per circle. Managers can pin reminders and announcements. Direct messages between members and manager. At-risk members are flagged for attention.

🔗

Blockchain Audit Trail

Every contribution, payout, and vouch bond is hashed to Polygon. Fully auditable and tamper-proof. Members see pesos and progress. Blockchain is the trust layer.

📍

Geo-Discovery

Find open circles near you. Trust is local — joining a circle with people in your community makes accountability real and personal. Filter by type, amount, and duration.

🏢

White Label Entities

Cooperatives, NGOs, and financial groups can operate their own branded CircleFi instance with their own circles, managers, analytics, and role structure.
